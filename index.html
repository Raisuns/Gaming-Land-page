Hoyoverse Company

2011, Universitas Jiao Tong Shanghai, Shanghai, Tiongkok

Hoyoverse is committed to providing immersive virtual world experiences to players around the world. Some of the popular games developed by HoYoverse are: Genshin Impact, Honkai: Star Rail, Honkai Impact 3, Tears of Themis, Zenless Zone Zero.

Honkai: Star Rail

26 April 2023

Honkai: Star Rail is an upcoming Open World RPG game with a Turnbase strategy battle system developed by miHoYo. It was first announced during the Honkai Impact 3rd live concert at the end of the event and along with the announcement of A POST HONKAI ODDYSEY 2.

Genshin Impact

28 September 2020

Genshin Impact begins in a world called Teyvat, which consists of seven great nations. These seven great nations are ruled by different gods called "Archons", who are associated with elements and ideals, and are commanded by a special faction. There is a floating island called Celestia above Teyvat, which is said to be the residence of gods or goddesses, along with creatures who have become gods because of their greatness and valor.

Honkai Impact 3rd

14 Oktober 2016

Honkai Impact 3rd is the third installment in the Honkai Series and a continuation of Houkai Gakuen, using many of the same characters from the previous titles, with a separate new story. The game is known for combining elements from various genres, from hack-and-slash, social stimulation, bullet hell, platforming, shoot 'em up, and dungeon crawling, found in both single player and multiplayer modes.

Zenless Zone Zero

4 Juli 2024

Zenless Zone Zero is an urban fantasy action role-playing game. Players take on the role of a Proxy (with the protagonist named Wise or Belle), a character who explores an alternate dimension known as the Hollows. Along the way, the Proxy will recruit new members while battling Ethereals and other enemies. In addition to the Proxy, players also use a robot assistant called Bangboo.
